How to Take Smart Notes

by Sönke Ahrens

Overview

How to Take Smart Notes by Sönke Ahrens introduces the Zettelkasten note-taking method, which is designed to help you build a knowledge system that encourages creativity, deep learning, and productivity. The book guides readers through the practical steps of taking notes, how to organize them for future use, and how this method can enhance academic writing and everyday learning.

Key Concepts

  • Zettelkasten Method: A note-taking system that creates connections between ideas.
  • Fleeting Notes: Short, quick notes taken during reading or thinking, intended to capture initial thoughts.
  • Permanent Notes: Thoughtful, elaborated notes that are written in your own words and connected to other ideas.
  • Project Notes: Notes directly related to specific projects or tasks.
  • Non-linear Thinking: A key benefit of the Zettelkasten system that allows for exploring ideas through interconnected notes.

Fun Fact

The Zettelkasten method was famously used by sociologist Niklas Luhmann, who attributed much of his productivity and over 70 published books to this note-taking system!
