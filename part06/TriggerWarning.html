Trigger Warning

by Neil Gaiman

Overview

Trigger Warning: Short Fictions and Disturbances is a collection of short stories and poems by Neil Gaiman, published in 2015. It includes tales of varying genres, from horror to science fiction to fantasy, exploring dark and unsettling themes that reflect human nature, mythology, and the unknown. The collection also features a novella set in the American Gods universe.

Notable Stories

  • The Truth Is a Cave in the Black Mountains: A story of greed, revenge, and the supernatural.
  • Orange: A humorous and surreal story about an alien invasion.
  • The Thing About Cassandra: A story about imaginary friends and their consequences.
  • A Calendar of Tales: A collection of short stories inspired by the months of the year.
  • Black Dog: A novella set in the American Gods universe.

Fun Fact

Neil Gaiman's "Trigger Warning" was nominated for the British Fantasy Award for Best Collection in 2016.
