Strange Chemistry

by Steven Farmer

Overview

Strange Chemistry is a fascinating exploration of the science behind chemical reactions and how they shape the world around us. Written by Steven Farmer, the book dives into both the history and the cutting-edge discoveries in the world of chemistry. It covers a wide range of topics, including the chemistry of everyday substances, chemical reactions that define life, and the mysteries that still elude scientists.

Key Chapters

  • Chapter 1: The Alchemy of Elements
  • Chapter 3: The Chemistry of Life
  • Chapter 6: Strange Reactions
  • Chapter 9: Chemistry of the Cosmos
  • Chapter 12: Chemical Mysteries Unsolved

Fun Fact

Strange Chemistry covers both historical discoveries and the modern-day challenges that chemists face in solving the mysteries of chemical reactions.
